About

I am a PhD student at The University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Lequan Yu. Previously, I obtained a BSc degree from the same university in 2024.

My research interests include: (1) Agentic AI: Autonomous agents capable of continual learning from experience and self-reflective reasoning; (2) Trustworthy Multimodal LLMs: Interpretable, robust, and uncertainty-aware multimodal LLMs; (3) Medical AI: Evidence-grounded, precise, and proactive AI for healthcare.
