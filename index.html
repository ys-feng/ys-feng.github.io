Biography

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at the School of Computing and Data Science, The University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Lequan Yu and Prof. Guodong Li. Prior to this, I received my B.Sc. degree in Decision Analytics with a minor in Finance from HKU in 2024.

My research aims to understand the intelligence to benefit the humanity with Deep Learning. Specifically, I focus on the following topics: (1) Large multimodal models; (2) Agentic system; (3) Generalist medical AI; (4) Graph learning.
